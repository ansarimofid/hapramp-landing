Blockchain Based Social Media
An Incentivized Social Media which rewards cryptocurrency for posting your work which is valuable to the community. HapRamp is for all the Creative heads be it photographer, musician, painter or writer.

Features

Community

Join like minded community of creators in each of your Interest areas. Create, curate, contribute and grow together.

Decentralized

On HapRamp, you own your content. Dapp provides you with Inbuilt Attribution, No Censorship, and Security.

Sweat Equity

Your hard work and contribution makes the platform grow, Sweat equity assures you get back a share of monetary value you add.

Contest

Participate in live competitions to earn great monetary rewards with each post. Top contributors will get a chance to create new competitions.

Hapcoin

Earn

Earn Cryptocurrency Reward ‘Hapcoins’ based on the ratings on your post.

Participate

Pay in Hapcoins to participate in contests and win Hapcoins or amazing prices.

Learn

Pay using HapCoins to learn new skills in the standardized HapRamp Studios.

Store

Buy community curated unique products from HapStore using HapCoins.

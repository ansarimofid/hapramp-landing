Blockchain Based Social Media
A rewards social media where you can earn for posting your work which helps the community. HapRamp is for all the Creative heads be it photographer, musician or painter

Features

Community

Relevant, clean, and useful feed only, Created and Curated by the community, for the community.

Decentralized

On HapRamp, you own your content. Dapp provides you with Inbuilt Attribution, No Censorship, and Security.

Sweat Equity

Get rewarded in Hapcoin for the ratings on your work by the community. Sweat equity is as valuable as cash equity.

Contest

Participate in contests and Win unique prices, or host competition and promote your products as a price.

Hapcoin

Earn

Earn in Hapcoins as you get ratings on the content you post in the channels.

Participate

Pay in Hapcoins to participate in contests and win Hapcoins or amazing prices.

Learn

Pay using HapCoins to learn new skills in the standardized HapRamp Studios.

Store

Buy community curated unique products from HapStore using HapCoins.

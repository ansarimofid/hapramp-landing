Blockchain Based Social Media
Universe of the creators across the globe, with the community in the center. All that you need to share, earn, and learn; A platform, currency, studios, store, and whatever the community will ask for. Your Ramp to happiness!

Features

Community

Dedicated channels for different Fine-Arts, join as per your interest. Giving relevant, clean, and useful experience of a true social media to the users.

Decentralized

We understand that you own your content. The Blockchain Technology provides inbuilt attribution, Zerocensorship, and security.

Sweat Equity

The effort users put in to create and post creative content on a platform brings value to the community. The concept of Sweat Equity enable creators to earn for their content.

Sweat Contest

Participate in challenging contests and win amazing prices. There are tools to create your own clubs, contests, and promote products as a price.

Hapcoin

Earn

Earn in Hapcoins as you get ratings on the content you post in the channels.

Participate

Pay in Hapcoins to participate in contests and win Hapcoins or amazing prices.

Learn

Pay using HapCoins to learn new skills in the standardized HapRamp Studios.

Store

Buy community curated unique products from HapStore using HapCoins.

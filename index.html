Social media that pays you
Steem-Blockchain powered social media that rewards you for sharing value with the community. It's time you stop giving your creative work for free, turn your likes into money on HapRamp.

What's different?

Community

Join creative communities of your interest and see only what you want on your feed.

Rewards

You Earn money (Steem Dollars) when others like your posts, they earn when you do.

Contests

Compete with your fellow community in interesting live contests and earn rewards.

And more...

Join the Decentralize revolution, own your content, and earn by doing what you love.

What to do with rewards?

Withdraw

You can withdraw your earned Steem Dollars into your bank account.

Transfer

Or send and receive Steem Dollars from your Steem wallet.

Compete

Use Steem tokens to join contests and win exciting prizes.

Hapstore

Use your Steem tokens on the HapStore to buy community-curated unique products.

FAQs
  • What is 1ramp?

    1ramp is a social media platform for creators. On signing up, you can join communities of your interest from the nine different creative communities (Literature, Photography, Fashion, Travel etc). You can share your work on 1ramp and browse through the communities to see what others are creating.

  • How do I signup on 1ramp?

    1ramp is built on top of the open-source Steem Blockchain. Any Steem user can directly sign in on 1ramp with the same username and password. If you are new to the Steem ecosystem, you’ll need to sign up on steem first. Then use the same username and password to signup on 1ramp or on any steem based platform. Click here to Sign up on Steem

  • What is Steem?

    Steem is an open-source Blockchain which can be used to build decentralized and incentivized content publishing platforms. The popular platforms built on Steem are Steemit, Dtube, and Busy.org. Learn more about Steem-Blockchain

  • How do I earn rewards on my posts?

    You’ll earn Steem Dollars and Steem Power based on the upvotes you receive on your posts. These are cryptocurrency tokens that carry real monetary value and can be exchanged with fiat currency. It is a new and easier way for the creators to monetize their work/content.

  • Is it safe to signup on 1ramp with my Steem Username and Password?

    1ramp utilizes steemconnect-v2 for user authorization and authentication. With this process, there are no means by which your personal information is collected. Steemconnect is the standard for user authentication on Steem blockchain.

  • Does it cost anything to post, comment, or rate?

    No, 1ramp is completely free to use. There are no paid subscriptions or tools on 1ramp.

  • Is there a limit to the number of blog/posts I can make in a day?

    Yes, the Steem blockchain has a limitation that a user cannot publish a new blog/post for five minutes immediately after publishing a blog/post on 1ramp.

  • What are Steem Dollars, Steem, and Steem-Power?

    There are three kinds of tokens on Steem: Steem, Steem-power (SP), and Steem-dollars (SBD). Users earn SP and SBD on their posts. SP is a measure of your influence on the platform, which will result in your own vote value. SBD is the actual earning which can be transferred, liquidated, or used for online shopping. Steem is a native currency to the Blockchain. Learn more about Steem-Blockchain Cryptocurrencies.

  • Can I make cryptocurrency transactions using the 1ramp Android app?

    No, this function is not yet available. For now, you can only see your wallet balances, curreny exchange rates of the tokens and transaction history. You may make transactions by accessing your wallet on Steemit.

  • I need help, how can I contact the 1ramp team for support?

    Join us on Discord and share the issues you are facing on the 1ramp app. You may also write us at help@1ramp.io.

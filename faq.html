FAQs
  • What is HapRamp?

    HapRamp is a social media platform for creators. On signing up, you can join communities of your interest from the nine different creative communities (Literature, Photography, Fashion, Travel etc). You can share your work on HapRamp and browse through the communities to see what others are creating.

  • How do I signup on HapRamp?

    HapRamp is built on top of the open-source Steem Blockchain. Any Steem user can directly sign in on HapRamp with the same username and password. If you are new to the Steem ecosystem, you’ll need to sign up on steem first. Then use the same username and password to use any steem based platform. Click here to Sign up on Steem

  • What is Steem?

    Steem is an open-source Blockchain which can be used to build decentralized and incentivized content publishing platforms. The popular platforms built on Steem are Steemit, Dtube, and Busy.org. Learn more about Steem-Blockchain

  • How do I earn rewards on my posts?

    You’ll earn Steem Dollars and Steem Power based on the upvotes you’ll receive on your posts. These are cryptocurrency tokens carry real monetary value and can be exchanged with fiat currency. It is a new and easier way for the creators to monetize their content.

  • What are Steem Dollars, Steem, and Steem-Power?

    There are three kinds of tokens on Steem: Steem, Steem-power (SP), and Steem-dollars (SBD). Users earn SP and SBD on their posts. SP is a measure of your influence on the platform, which will result in your own vote value. SBD is the actual earning which can be transferred, liquidated, or used for online shopping. Steem is a native currency to the Blockchain. Learn more about Steem-Blockchain Cryptocurrencies.

  • Where can I get the HapRamp App?

    HapRamp Android app is not launched yet. We are currently testing the platform in closed groups. Can’t wait for the launch, join our Discord, get an early access, and give us your valuable feedback. Keep an eye on HapRamp, it’ll be available anytime soon!

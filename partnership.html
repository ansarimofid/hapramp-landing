Official Community Management Program

HapRamp platform is for nine creative communities to help them join the Steem ecosystem.

We Invite partnerships from individuals/organizations who have influence on the network and actively support the creators on Steem. For example, a partner for our Photography Domain will manage that community: curating valuable content, helping artists gain traction and earn rewards, and collaborate with the Hapramp team to improve the platform.

The partners will have access to our Community fund* to manage and put it to good use.

Each partner shall get 30% of the entire benefactor reward earned in their particular domain.

Initiatives by HapRamp

Building a platform that creators need

Dedicated channels for different Fine-Arts, join as per your interest. Giving relevant, clean, and useful experience of a true social media to the users.

HapFolks

HapFolks is our upcoming project to help the hyperlocal artist communities who need tech support to position themselves or their organisation on the Internet. This will be free of cost. The idea is to bestow enough resources that every artist could create content and gets a channel to monetize their hard work and talent.

Community support fund

We are creating a generous fund which will be 10% of the benefactor reward on Hapramp + any donations that we receive. This fund will be managed by Hapramp along with our community partner with complete transparency. This fund shall be used to conduct meetups, events and support artists.
